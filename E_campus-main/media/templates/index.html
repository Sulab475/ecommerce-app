About Our Institution

🏛️ Our History

Established in 2008, Technique Polytechnic Institute has been a pioneer in technical education in the region. What started as a vision to provide quality polytechnic education has grown into one of the most respected technical institutions.

Over the years, we have produced thousands of skilled technicians and engineers who are now contributing to various industries across the nation and abroad.
